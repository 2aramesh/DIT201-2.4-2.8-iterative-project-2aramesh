Community Service

Gear List:

Give back with Purpose!

Community Service is a rewarding and meaningful way to give back through
hands-on support and local involvement. From assisting with gardening,
painting, or clean-up projects to helping out at local charities, early
childhood centres, or support organisations, the tasks are varied,
purposeful, and always impactful. Rooted in the spirit of empathy,
connection and citizenship, this activity strengthens ties within
the community and fosters a culture of care, pride, and shared
responsibility. It's a chance to contribute time and effort to real
needs while making a visible difference in the lives of others.

Key Information:

Students will complete one day of community service assisting local residents and businesses. This will usually occur within walking distance of Tawa College for all Year 10 students. This day has the key focus of citizenship and has been a great success in previous years.

Students will spend a day completing community service in a small group with friends in the Tawa basin, supervised by a staff member.
